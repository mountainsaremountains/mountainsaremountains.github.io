The journey to understanding any subject is one that requires motivation and study. I have spent over ten years studying physics. I am here to help you on your path to understanding physics. I approach problems by relating difficult concepts to experiences we all have. I help students when they get stuck by asking questions so they can find the answer. Once the answer is found, I help students check it to make sure that it is correct. This process is essential to developing a full and deep understanding of physics.

Policies

Tutor Expectations

I prepare lessons for students by discussing with the student to tailor a program that will most benefit the student. Most of the time this means preparing problems that follow a course syllabus, but can include reviewing past material depending on student interest or ability. Occasionally lessons will include a significant portion for the student to ask questions about past material or help with homework problems that are not graded for a course.

I structure my lessons around having the student solve problems. Much of physics is learned by working through problems, and my tutoring sessions are no different. I emphasize techniques over memorizing formulas. I work with students and guide them to the solution through question and answer. This is in contrast to a lecture hall where the results are presented as if by magic, and the reasoning is sometimes glossed over. This is however time intensive, and sometimes it can require multiple sessions to cover a topic if the student is struggling with a particular concept.

Student Expectations

I expect that students arrive on time and ready to learn. If a student arrives late, I charge half the hourly rate until the student arrives (or the entire rate if the student is a no-show).

Every student is different and arrives at a different level. I recognize this and adjust my tutoring strategy accordingly. At times I prepare more material than can be covered in the time. In that case I send the student home with the extra material and cover it again the next week. I work with students to prepare study guides for upcoming tests and finals. This is for the students benefit, and if time permits I will look over student solutions in order to assess progress.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your tutoring session with no penalty up to 24 hours before the scheduled session. If, however, you cancel within 24 hours of the session, payment is due in full. To cancel an appointment, you can call, email or text my cell phone.

Guarantee/Liability

I provide no guarantees of my tutoring services whatsoever as far as performance on exams quizzes or homework. The reason being that performance is based solely on the student's hard work and investment in the material.
